About us

Our Story

A&S Home Design is a family-run bespoke German kitchen business based in Kirkintilloch with over 30 years of experience. Founded by Nephew/Uncle team, Stephen and Adrian McGuigan, they create stunning and efficient kitchens that add value to your home. They also supply and install Nobilia German kitchens which are well-known for their high quality and rigid build. Their family values, laid back customer approach, and their stunning attention to detail make them stand out in a world of cold, sales driven kitchen showrooms.

Business Challenge

Business Challenge

A&S Home Design lacked time to focus on a concrete marketing strategy, including posting to social media on a regular basis. They wanted to have more people visit their showroom and grow their revenues past what their reliance on word-of-mouth marketing has already done for them.

What we did

What we did

We carried out a variety of activities to help aid A&S Home Design’s business goals, starting with posting to more social media accounts, including Instagram, and much more regularly. We also boosted our efforts and spent more time to increase engagement and following on Facebook and Instagram, even running some Facebook ads campaigns.

To increase awareness among current leads and prospects, we carried out monthly email marketing campaigns. We also built a brand-new website with eCommerce, a showroom booking system, and a chatbot integrated into it. To support the new website and its content, we carried out a number of photo and video shoots at their showroom in Kirkintilloch. We also created an easy way for customer testimonial requests to be sent, reviews to be gathered, and finally posted.

Overall Impact

Overall Impact

The impact of what we did for A&S Home Design was a 300% increase in their social media following, a massive rise in leads, visits to the showroom increasing significantly, and the team being booked up 4 months in advance for residential kitchen installations.

Social Media

Before our service started, A&S Home Design only used Facebook and LinkedIn to promote their business. When we started providing our service, we introduced Twitter, Instagram, along with Google MyBusiness to help build up a larger variety of platforms in which awareness can be built among a varied audience. The regular posting schedule also immediately eliminated one of the customer’s issues, not having the time to post to social media.

Social Media Boost Service

As part of the Facebook and Instagram boost services which we offered our customer, we carried out specific manual tasks every week to help gather more followers, create extended visibility in relevant groups, and drive more engagement overall.

Facebook Ads

We also set up and ran Facebook Ads as a source of paid lead gen. These campaigns were successful and saw a good return, getting more customers to visit the showroom allowing for a sales dialogue to be had with the prospective customer.

Email Marketing

We provided an email marketing service in the form of a monthly email newsletter that would build awareness among prospects and previous customers alike. This helped to generate more website traffic and interest in A&S Home Design’s services.

New Website

The previous A&S Home Design website was built on Squarespace to be easy for one person to update. It was now outdated and needed to be updated to meet the customer’s new business goals, updated products, and to incorporate eCommerce. The new website has been built on WordPress and now loads much faster, thanks to the optimisation we carried out. It also looks more up to date with the customer’s branding, bringing in the correct colour schemes and fonts to support this. It is also far more functional as both a sales and marketing tool, has a clear SEO strategy behind it and is structured to be far more user friendly than the previous site.

ECommerce Integration

With the new website now built, we integrated eCommerce into the site, allowing the customer to increase their revenue through online sales of flat packed kitchens, worktops, flooring, and other lower value items.

Online Chatbot

An online chatbot was also added to the site to help provide value for potential customers, answering any questions they might have. The chatbot can also allow customers to request a digital copy of A&S Home Design’s kitchen brochure and to book an appointment at the showroom.

Photography and
Videography services

To support the website and social media with high-quality content, we carried out a number of photoshoots at A&S Home Design’s showroom in Kirkintilloch and the intention is to do more top end photography around A&S Home Design’s completed customer kitchens. The photos and videos we have taken were able to support a number of different marketing activities which include keeping social media posts up to date, updating social media page banners, maintaining the new website, and running ads campaigns.

Customer Testimonial
Request Process

To help support A&S Home Design’s already strong word-of-mouth marketing efforts, we built a system that allowed the team to send customer testimonials requests to the customers via a custom branded landing page. It then directs the customer to preferred review channels such as Facebook, google, and repuso, where they can rate and comment on the service they were provided. The team at A&S Home Design can then manually approve the review, after which it will automatically appear on their website without the need to do anything else.
