About us

Our Story

Based only 20 mins outside of Glasgow in their native Ayrshire town of Kilmarnock, Ayrshire Agencies are a Commercial and Residential glazing company with more than 25 years’ experience in their industry. Started by Cecil Wilson in the 1990s, the company continues to be family run today by his son, David. The company operates a trade counter and factory where they manufacture products for their own use, and to be sold to fellow tradespeople. The team at Ayrshire Agencies take pride in the quality of work they carry out for their residential, commercial, and trade customers, and are renowned as far south as the north of England for their attention to detail and commitment to the job at hand.

Business Challenge

Business Challenge

Ayrshire Agencies lacked the time to focus on solid marketing efforts, relying heavily on word of mouth for most of their work. Before contacting Autus, they had a Facebook page which saw very little in terms of posting, and only ever occasionally posted on LinkedIn. It was clear that Ayrshire Agencies needed to leverage more marketing channels to reach a wider audience if they wanted to reach their business goals.

What we did

What we did

To help achieve business goals for Ayrshire Agencies, we first carried out Social Media management. Not only increasing their posting to Facebook and LinkedIn but also to Twitter to expand their reach and begin leveraging more channels. In conjunction with social media management, we carried out Facebook Ads to help drive awareness and enquiries. We also implemented the free version of Hubspot CRM and a slick integration with MailChimp to allow the team to keep track of prospects and leads during their sales and marketing processes. Email marketing was next on the agenda, with monthly emails being sent out to previous customers, leads, and prospects alike to help drive more awareness and sales enquiries. We also helped design an operational/production calendar system, optimised sales process and carried out Marketing Consultancy sessions, providing extra value and coming up with a solidified marketing strategy to drive for big business goals.

Overall Impact

Overall Impact

The impact that our services had on Ayrshire Agencies was night-and-day. Engagement on social media increased massively with more locals being made aware of the services that the business offered. Awareness among previous customers and new leads also increased significantly. With our on-going marketing consultancy, we were able to continually raise the bar to help hit set business goals.

Social Media
Management

Before coming to Autus, Ayrshire Agencies only occasionally used LinkedIn and never posted on their Facebook page to raise awareness for their business. When we started our service, we immediately started regularly posting 3 times a week across Facebook, LinkedIn business, LinkedIn personal, and added Twitter as another source of organic awareness. The difference was almost immediately obvious as engagement across all channels went up dramatically. The amount of website traffic being received as a result of social media posting also went up significantly.

Facebook Ads

Alongside our social media management service, we also ran Facebook Ads as a source of paid lead gen. This especially helped with a new product market launch during the height of Covid, whereby we helped Ayrshire Agencies pivot in promoting glass protection screens to a range of businesses. These campaigns were successful and saw return on investment through higher audience awareness, an increase in engagement and sales growth.

Hubspot and Mailchimp Implementation and Integration

We implemented and integrated HubSpot CRM and MailChimp systems into Ayrshire Agencies’ marketing and sales workflow. This allowed the team to keep track of leads, build up a database of contacts, and even run their own email marketing campaigns. This integration helped streamline and modernise the company’s previous workflow which heavily relied on manual processes and took up valuable time.

Email Marketing

In an effort to raise more awareness for Ayrshire Agencies’ different services, we ran monthly email marketing campaigns. These emails included general industry news and information around the different products and services that the company offers its residential and trade customers. After starting this part of the service, we saw a monthly increase in visitors to the Ayrshire Agencies website along with a rise in enquiry submissions.

Marketing Consultancy

Our relationship with Ayrshire Agencies has extended many years and Autus has become a go to ‘trusted advisor’ for end-to-end marketing consultancy for the business. Examples of having to advise on new website construction, integration and future marketing activities that can boost the business success further, are second nature to the day to day dialogue had with the business owner and key personnel.
